## 📌 Project Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing $118M+ in global financial sales data across 5 countries, 6 products, and 5 business segments from September 2013 to December 2014. Built to demonstrate end-to-end BI development — from raw Excel data to executive-ready visual reporting.

## 💡 Key Business Insights
- France is the most profitable country ($3.78M profit)
- Paseo is the top product by profit ($4.79M)
- Sales grew 249% from 2013 to 2014
- Enterprise segment is loss-making (-$614K)
- Government segment contributes 67% of total profit
- $9.2M in discounts impacted overall margins

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards — Total Revenue, Profit, Units Sold, Margin
- Country-wise profit map (5 countries)
- Product performance comparison chart
- Segment profitability analysis (P&L view)
- Discount band impact on revenue
- Year-over-Year sales growth (2013 vs 2014)
- Interactive slicers — filter by Country, Segment, Year
